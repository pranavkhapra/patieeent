Online doctor appointment system.

Online doctor appointment sysytem is a non-profitable platform created and mantained by a team of student volunteers of Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology that seeks to provide affordable access to doctors to the remote parts of the country. We provide online diagnostics with valid prescriptions so patients can buy the drugs and other healthcare they need.

We are based in Patiala,punjab.
